## STYLE-01 — Strategy Consulting

**Vibe:** McKinsey, BCG, Bain. Data-first, no-nonsense. Every element earns its place.

- **Fonts:** Georgia (headings, bold) + Calibri (body). No decorative fonts.
- **Colors:** Navy `#0B1D3A` headings, `#1A1A1A` body, `#999999` muted, `#003DA5` accent (royal blue), white page bg.
- **Margins:** 1" all sides (standard).
- **Cover:** Classic Corporate pattern. Centered title, thin blue accent bar, clean metadata below.
- **Tables:** Professional Banded. Blue header row + white text. Alternating `#F5F5F5` rows. Hairline borders only.
- **Decorative:** Thin blue underline below H1. No shadows, no 3D effects. Horizontal rules between sections. Monochrome throughout.
- **Callouts:** Light gray bg `#F8F9FA` + left blue border. Data callout cards for KPIs.
- **Design notes:** Restrained. Max 2 colors visible on any page. No images except charts. White space is your friend.

## STYLE-02 — Executive Editorial

**Vibe:** Harvard Business Review, The Economist. Thought-leadership elegance.

- **Fonts:** Rockwell (headings, bold) + Georgia (body). Italic Georgia for pull quotes.
- **Colors:** Crimson `#C8102E` headings, `#333333` body, `#8C8279` muted, cream page accents `#FAF7F2`, warm white bg.
- **Margins:** Wide — 1.25" left/right, 1" top/bottom.
- **Cover:** Minimal Elegant pattern. Maximum whitespace, crimson accent underline, author info at page bottom.
- **Tables:** Accent-Top Minimal. Crimson top border, no vertical lines, clean horizontal separators.
- **Decorative:** Crimson pull quotes (large italic Georgia). Short accent bars under headings. Generous paragraph spacing.
- **Callouts:** Warm cream bg `#FAF7F2` + crimson left border. Used for key takeaways.
- **Design notes:** Generous whitespace. Crimson accents sparingly — one per page max. Sidebar callouts for key points.

## STYLE-03 — Creative Brief

**Vibe:** Sketch-pad, brainstorm, agency creative. Loose, informal, energetic.

- **Fonts:** Comic Sans MS (headings, bold) + Calibri (body). Italic Calibri for annotations.
- **Colors:** Dark pencil `#3C3C3C` headings, `#3C3C3C` body, `#A0A0A0` muted, rust orange `#D46A3C` accent, ink blue `#2B4570` accent2, kraft bg `#F5F0E1`.
- **Margins:** Narrow — 0.75" all sides (more canvas).
- **Cover:** Side-Accent pattern. Rust orange vertical bar + hand-drawn feel title.
- **Tables:** Thin borders only, no fills. Sketchy feel — 1pt `#A0A0A0` borders.
- **Decorative:** Dashed horizontal rules. Annotation-style italic notes in margins. Irregular spacing (deliberate).
- **Callouts:** Kraft bg `#F5F0E1` + thin pencil-grey border. Handwritten-feel labels.
- **Design notes:** Loose layout. Nothing perfectly aligned. Slightly asymmetric margins. Annotation labels everywhere.

## STYLE-04 — Playful / Kawaii

**Vibe:** Kid-friendly, lifestyle brand, fun product docs. Bubbly, colorful, rounded.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headings, bold) + Calibri (body). Rounded, friendly feel.
- **Colors:** Deep plum `#4A2040` headings, `#4A2040` body, `#A0A0A0` muted. Rotating pastel accents: pink `#FFD6E0`, lavender `#E8D5F5`, mint `#D5F5E3`. Peach `#FFAB91`, sky `#81D4FA`.
- **Margins:** Standard 1" but generous internal spacing.
- **Cover:** Bold Banner pattern but with pastel pink bg + white text. Rounded callout box for subtitle.
- **Tables:** Pastel header (rotating colors per table). Rounded-feel cells (extra padding). No harsh borders.
- **Decorative:** Rounded callout boxes. Pastel accent bars. Alternating section backgrounds (rotate pastels). Generous padding everywhere.
- **Callouts:** Pastel bg (rotating) + slightly darker border. Pill-shaped feel.
- **Design notes:** Generous padding. Soft colors. Alternate pastel bg across sections. Never harsh or angular.

## STYLE-05 — Corporate Modern

**Vibe:** Apple, Stripe, modern SaaS. Clean, precise, systematic.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headings, bold) + Calibri (body). Systematic, no-fuss.
- **Colors:** Near-black `#1D1D1F` headings, `#1D1D1F` body, `#6E6E73` muted, electric blue `#0071E3` accent, `#D1D1D6` borders, white bg, alt bg `#F8F9FA`.
- **Margins:** Standard 1".
- **Cover:** Classic Corporate pattern. Blue accent bar, clean hierarchy, logo placement.
- **Tables:** Professional Banded. Blue header, `#F8F9FA` alt rows. Subtle `#D1D1D6` borders.
- **Decorative:** Thin blue underlines under H1. Card-style callouts with subtle borders. KPI cards in table rows. Systematic spacing (12pt body, 24pt before headings).
- **Callouts:** White bg + `#D1D1D6` border + blue left accent. Clean, card-like.
- **Design notes:** 12-column-grid thinking. Card-based sections. Hero metrics large. Subtle shadows (via borders). Systematic everything.

## STYLE-06 — Bold Narrative

**Vibe:** Cinematic, dramatic, story-driven. Annual reports, brand stories.

- **Fonts:** Arial Black (headings) + Calibri (body). Large dramatic titles.
- **Colors:** Deep twilight `#1A1A40` for dark elements, warm highlight `#FFF8E1`, cerulean `#1E88E5`, rose `#E91E63`, amber `#FFB300`. White page bg.
- **Margins:** Wide — 1.25" left/right. Room to breathe.
- **Cover:** Bold Banner pattern. Dark navy banner + white/amber title. Dramatic presence.
- **Tables:** Dark header `#1A1A40` + white text. Clean horizontal rules only. No vertical borders.
- **Decorative:** Large pull quotes in amber. Thick accent bars (4pt) above sections. Dark sidebar panels. Bold color blocks for emphasis.
- **Callouts:** Dark navy bg `#1A1A40` + amber text/border. Inverted for drama.
- **Design notes:** Wide cinematic feel. Rich atmospheric colors. Large dramatic titles (28-36pt). Minimal body text per page. Dark elements create contrast.

## STYLE-07 — Warm Organic

**Vibe:** Sustainability, wellness, nature, artisan. Earthy, gentle, grounded.

- **Fonts:** Cambria (headings, bold) + Calibri (body). Warm serif + clean sans.
- **Colors:** Deep brown `#2D1B0E` headings, `#44382C` body, `#8B7355` muted, terracotta `#C2704E` accent, sage green `#7C9A6E` accent2, warm cream bg hints `#FDF8F0`.
- **Margins:** Standard 1" with generous paragraph spacing.
- **Cover:** Minimal Elegant pattern. Terracotta accent line, earthy tones, natural imagery.
- **Tables:** Accent-Top Minimal. Terracotta top border. No harsh grid. Warm alt-row `#FDF8F0`.
- **Decorative:** Terracotta horizontal rules. Green accent for positive elements. Warm cream callout boxes. Rounded corners on images.
- **Callouts:** Cream bg `#FEF7ED` + terracotta left border. Warm, inviting.
- **Design notes:** Generous negative space. Warm tones throughout. Natural feel — nothing harsh or angular. Two-color accent system (terracotta + sage).

## STYLE-08 — Magazine Editorial

**Vibe:** Vogue, Monocle, Bloomberg Businessweek. Asymmetric, bold, typographic.

- **Fonts:** Georgia (headings, bold, 28-36pt) + Georgia (body). Pullquotes in italic Georgia.
- **Colors:** Near-black `#0A0A0A` headings, `#0A0A0A` body, `#757575` muted, yellow `#FFD600` accent, magenta `#E91E63` accent2, cobalt `#1A237E` accent3.
- **Margins:** Asymmetric — 1.5" left, 1" right. Magazine bleed feel.
- **Cover:** Bold Banner pattern with massive title (48pt+). High contrast. One bold accent color.
- **Tables:** Minimal. Thick top rule `#0A0A0A`, thin bottom rule. No internal borders. Caption below in muted.
- **Decorative:** Massive headlines (48pt+). Thick divider rules (3-4pt). Bold statement accent per section. Pull quotes in yellow with thick borders.
- **Callouts:** Black bg `#0A0A0A` + white text (inverted) or yellow bg + black text. Maximum contrast.
- **Design notes:** Asymmetric grid. High contrast. One bold statement per page. Thick divider rules. Mix huge headlines with small body.

## STYLE-09 — Technical Documentation

**Vibe:** API docs, engineering specs, developer guides. Structured, scannable, precise.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headings, bold) + Calibri (body). Consolas for code blocks.
- **Colors:** Dark gray `#2C2C2C` headings, `#2C2C2C` body, `#757575` muted, red `#E53935` accent, yellow `#FFEB3B` highlight, warm white bg `#F0EDE8` for code.
- **Margins:** Standard 1".
- **Cover:** Classic Corporate pattern. Version number prominent. Clean hierarchy. No decorative imagery.
- **Tables:** Professional Banded with thin borders. Code-style cells (Consolas, small padding).
- **Decorative:** Numbered section markers in red. Code blocks with warm-white bg + thin border. Annotation strips for important notes. Thin horizontal rules.
- **Callouts:** Three types: Note (blue left border), Warning (yellow left border + yellow bg tint), Error/Important (red left border + red bg tint).
- **Design notes:** Scannable. Deep heading hierarchy (H1-H4). Generous use of code blocks. Numbered panels for step-by-step. No decorative images — diagrams only.

## STYLE-10 — Dashboard Report

**Vibe:** KPI dashboards, data reports, analytics summaries. Metric-forward, structured.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headings, bold) + Calibri (body). Large metrics in Calibri bold 36-48pt.
- **Colors:** Dark mode option: `#1C1C1E` elements on white page. Light mode: white bg with `#2C2C2E` cards. Blue `#0071E3`, green `#34C759`, purple `#AF52DE`, orange `#FF9F0A` for metric accents.
- **Margins:** Narrow 0.75" for maximum data density.
- **Cover:** Minimal — title + date + key metric hero (one big number).
- **Tables:** KPI Card Row pattern. Each cell is a card with thick colored top border. Banded data tables with narrow rows.
- **Decorative:** KPI cards prominent. Color-coded metrics (green up, red down). Thin section dividers. Dense but organized.
- **Callouts:** Card-style with colored top accent border. Background `#F5F5F7`. One card per metric.
- **Design notes:** Metric-forward. Each page tells a data story. Mixed card sizes. Color-coded everything. Dense but never cluttered — use grids.

## STYLE-11 — Portfolio / Gallery

**Vibe:** Photography, design portfolio, lookbook. Image-forward, minimal text.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headings, light/thin) + Calibri (body, small). Caption style.
- **Colors:** `#1A1A1A` headings, `#757575` body/captions, very light gray bg `#EAEAEA`, white card bg, red accent `#E60023` sparingly.
- **Margins:** Narrow 0.75" for maximum image area.
- **Cover:** Bold Banner with full-width hero image. Title overlay on image (via table cell with bg image).
- **Tables:** Invisible borders — used only for image grid layout. 2-3 columns of images.
- **Decorative:** Minimal. Let images speak. Thin captions below images. Generous image-to-image spacing. Rounded corners on all images.
- **Callouts:** Minimal text boxes. Thin left border + small italic caption. Never compete with images.
- **Design notes:** Images are the hero. 60%+ of page area is images. Small, elegant captions. Generous whitespace between items. All images get rounded corners.

## STYLE-12 — Retro / Vintage

**Vibe:** Indie zine, risograph, letterpress. Textured, limited palette, imperfect charm.

- **Fonts:** Rockwell (headings, bold) + Consolas (body — typewriter feel). Monospace for everything except headings.
- **Colors:** Dark navy `#1B2838` (NO pure black), riso blue `#0078BF`, riso red `#FF665E`, riso green `#00A95C`, uncoated paper `#F2E8D5` bg tint.
- **Margins:** Standard 1" with generous internal spacing.
- **Cover:** Side-Accent pattern. Thick riso-blue vertical bar. Bold Rockwell title. Paper-tone background.
- **Tables:** Thick borders in navy `#1B2838`. No fills — just strong lines. Typewriter body text.
- **Decorative:** Bold flat-color shapes. Overlapping color blocks (print overprint effect). Thick rules. Limited to 2-3 colors per page.
- **Callouts:** Paper bg `#F2E8D5` + thick navy border (all sides). Typewriter text inside.
- **Design notes:** Max 2-3 colors per spread. No pure black — use dark navy. Bold flat shapes. Overlap elements for overprint effect. Paper texture tone bg. Typewriter body creates the retro feel.
